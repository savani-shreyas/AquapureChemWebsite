about us

Aquapure Chem is a trusted manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of high-quality industrial chemicals based in North York, ON, Canada, BIN: 1001463671. With over 20 years of industry experience, the company specializes in delivering reliable chemical solutions to various industries including textiles, pharmaceuticals, food processing, water treatment, and chemical manufacturing.

Our product portfolio includes a wide range of acids, industrial chemicals, solvents, pigments, dyes, and industrial salts that meet international quality standards. We are committed to providing safe packaging, consistent quality, and timely delivery to customers across domestic and global markets.

At Aquapure Chem, we believe in building long-term partnerships by offering dependable products, competitive pricing, and excellent customer service. Our goal is to support industries with efficient chemical solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

our vision

To be a trusted leader in chemical and water treatment solutions.

our mission

Providing pure, reliable, and innovative chemical solutions.

our values
